About GDG NMIT

Google Developer Student Clubs, Nitte Meenakshi Institute of Technology is a college based community functioning with an aim to promote peer-to-peer learning with an active usage of tools and technologies offered by Google. Here in the community, we learn and further our developers' skills with an intent to abide by the idea of putting knowledge to practical use.
We, in the community, conduct webinars, hands-on workshops, tutorials, informative talks and more to engage with the audience and grow the community at large. To connect with us and help us promote the peer-to-peer learning culture,

GENAI Study Jams

Study jems, nov 11, 2024

A Gen AI Study Jam is an interactive, group-based learning session where participants dive into the world of generative AI, exploring its tools, techniques, and applications together. Hands-On Workshops: These sessions are filled with practical activities, such as coding labs, where attendees get to build generative AI models—like text generators or image creators—using tools like OpenAI's GPT or DALL·E. Project-Based Learning: Participants are often encouraged to work on small-scale projects that tackle real-world problems. This approach deepens their understanding of AI concepts by applying them in practice. Networking and Collaboration: Study Jams also provide an excellent opportunity for AI enthusiasts, developers, and professionals to connect, share knowledge, and collaborate, fostering a vibrant AI community. Recognition and Certification: Many events offer certificates or recognition for active participants, adding value to their professional profiles and showcasing their growing expertise in generative AI."

Build with AI

Info Session - GDG NMIT, Oct 17, 2024

In the GEN AI Study Jams session, you'll explore Generative AI using Google Cloud Platform (GCP). We’ll begin with an overview of the labs, detailing tasks, timelines, and key objectives to help you navigate the program effectively. You’ll learn to access and complete labs, engage with learning modules, and earn skill badges. We'll provide tips for overcoming challenges, address common questions, and emphasize the importance of timely completion. By the session’s end, you'll have a clear roadmap for success, prepared to develop practical GCP skills and confidently progress through each stage of this hands-on learning experience.
